# Contributing

Contributions are welcome.

By submitting a patch, pull request, or other contribution, you agree to the
following:

1. You have the right to submit the contribution.
2. Your contribution is voluntary and provided without expectation of payment,
   royalties, or other compensation unless a separate written agreement says
   otherwise.
3. You grant the project the right to use, copy, modify, merge, publish,
   distribute, sublicense, and incorporate your contribution as part of the
   Software under the repository license.
4. You do not gain ownership of the product, its name, or related assets by
   contributing.
5. Each contribution should include a `Signed-off-by:` line in the commit
   message or patch footer, in the spirit of the Developer Certificate of
   Origin.

Please keep changes focused and easy to review.
